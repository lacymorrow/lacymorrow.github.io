## About

> Complete web design & development. Developed custom WordPress theme. Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP, WordPress

Phase2 Productions is a full-service video production company based in Charlotte, NC. They needed a website that would showcase their work and allow them to update their portfolio on their own. I developed a custom WordPress theme for them that allowed them to easily add new videos and photos to their portfolio.
